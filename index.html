Mona Bartling

My name is Mona and I am a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Zurich, Switzerland. I am recipient of the FWF funded Erwin-Schrödinger fellowship (see Ongoing Research). In my research I focus on inclusive geospatial data visualization, map use context, and map design adaptations.
