MONA BARTLING

Research • Cartography • Geography • Design

I am a PhD candidate and researcher at the Department of Geoinformatics - Z_GIS at the University of Salzburg. I have a strong research focus on map design usability, map use context, and map design adaptations. I have 5+ years work experiences in digital agriculture where I worked in various international research institutes.

I have a passion for cartographic design, and I am aiming to look at the margins of map design solutions. You can find my full CV here!

I enjoy hiking, yoga, and all sorts of water sports. I also collaborate with friends on FARO.design, where we create geographic-inspired t-shirt designs.
